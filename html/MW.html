Choose your means of transport Want to know the approximate flight times from the world's major travel hubs to Tenerife? Interested in knowing which of our airports have the most international connections, or our main cruise ports? To make sure you are completely prepared, we also answer some of your doubts on car hire or public transport to reach the city centres from the airports.

Map of airports

Passports are always required except for citizens of the European Union, Switzerland, United Kingdom, or countries that are members of the European Economic Area. In these cases, citizens can travel with a valid identity card (except for minors, who must have a parental permit).


We recommend that you arrange your rental vehicle before you travel and collect it at the airport upon arrival, at many train and bus stations or at the office agreed with the rental company. You can also book it directly at your destination. You can find car rental companies at all the airports and in the main train and bus stations.


If you need to change your money to €, you will be able to do so at bureaus de change, at various banks, and at the majority of hotels and travel agencies. You will normally find offices or ATMs that exchange currency in the international airports and high speed train stations.


Bus journeys are usually paid for upon climbing aboard and they usually only accept cash. Payment is always in € and change is usually limited (never above €20). Metro and train journeys are paid for at the station and there are usually ticket machines. On certain occasions, they only accept payment by credit or debit card.
