Explore the amazing nature of Tenerife

Tenerife is the largest of the Canary Islands. So, there is plenty of space to explore its beautiful nature.

Don’t be fooled by Tenerife’s gorgeous beaches. Tenerife’s beaches are actually manmade.
Tenerife’s natural beaches would be coloured black due to the volcano Teide. Teide is a very famous tourist attraction these days.
It is in fact the eighth most visited national park in the whole world reaching over 3 million visitors on yearly basics.
For nature lovers, hiking up the mountain is must to do. Great exercise to balance all the delicious food and breahtaking views.

Remember to pack a lot of light clothes and tons of sunscreen for your trip to Tenerife.

Sun is shining almost all year around and temperatures are usually always more than 20 C.

Remember to take many pictures of the beautiful nature of Teneri
