Whale and dolphin watching

Climb aboard for an unforgettable 3-hour cruise by fully equipped sailboat or yacht, and venture along the southern coast of Tenerife looking for whales and dolphins in their natural environment. Encounter up to 26 species known to inhabit the waters, including baleen whales, killer whales, dolphins, pilot whales and blue whales. Go to a private beach if you prefer and swim and snorkel amongst the island's unique flora and fauna. Relax on deck and laze in the sun as you avail of the free drinks, snacks and a meal. Rest assured by the “Blue Boat” flag that certifies the local operator's professional authorization to carry out these activities and ensures that the on-board staff know all about the mammals to help you understand their behavior better.

Siam park

Siam Park is the largest water park in Europe. The park in the south of Tenerife, in Adeje, has a really long network of impressive water slides. Within 185 km² of the park, visitors can surf on giant waves, glide down a really high pace or cross a shark-filled pool. Private Thai-style cottages are the perfect place to spend a pampering day. In Siam Park, adrenaline is unleashed.

Mountain biking

Mountain biking down the mountainside, through the cloud-covered canary pine forests, is suitable for wildlife. Bike tours can be arranged through local rental shops in the tourist centers. For example, the downhill from Teide National Park to Los Cristianos is 50 kilometers long and the difference in altitude for the adventure will be a whopping 3,000 meters!
